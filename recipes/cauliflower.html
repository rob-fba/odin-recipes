Easy Roasted Cauliflower

Description:

This crunchy, spicy roasted cauliflower is so tasty that even your kids will be delighted!

This recipe takes total 45 minutes and serves 4.

Ingradients:

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 head cauliflower, broken into florets
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp mild curry powder

The Steps:

  1. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Grease a baking sheet with 1 tsp olive oil.
  2. Combine cauliflower and 2 tbsp olive oil in a bowl. Mix brown sugar, salt and curry powder in a small bowl. Taste and Adjust mixture until it's sweet and salty. Sprinkle over cauliflower and mix well. Spread cauliflower in a single layer on the baking sheet.
  3. Roast in pre-heated oven for 20 minutes. Turn cauliflower and roast for another 15 to 20 minutes.
