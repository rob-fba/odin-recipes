Amazing Lasagna!

Description:

Classic lasagna with great taste with fast and simple recipe!

This recipe takes 30 minutes to prepare and 30 minutes to cook. It serves 8.

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet of lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 pound shredded chedder cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese

The Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain.
  2. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  3. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt & pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlic and simmer 5 minutes
  4. In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, chedder and ricotta; stir well. In a 9 * 13 inches pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  5. Bake in pre-heated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
