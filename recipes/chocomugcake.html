Chocolate Mug Cake

Description:

This yummy chocolaty treate takes less than 10 minutes to make!

Just stir together all the ingrediens in a cup and microwave. You have a treat!

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp canola oil

The Steps:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda and salt in a large microwave-safe mug; stir in milk, canola oil and vanilla extract.
  2. Cook in microwave until cake is cokked in the middle, about 1 minute 45 seconds.
